PRODUCT

Semi-detached

  • Definition: A house joined to another on one side only.
  • Ownership: Usually freehold.
  • Size: Typically 2–5 bedrooms, with a private garden.
  • Floors: Mostly 2-storey.
  • Privacy: Moderate – more than a terraced, less than detached.
  • Parking: Often has a driveway or garage.
  • Location: Common in suburban areas.
  • Appeal: Popular with families due to space and affordability.

Apartment

  • Definition: A self-contained housing unit within a building, usually part of a larger complex.
  • Ownership: Often leasehold, but can be freehold in some cases (e.g., condos).
  • Size: Varies, from studios to multi-bedroom units.
  • Floors: Located in multi-storey buildings.
  • Privacy: Limited, as walls are shared with neighbors.
  • Parking: May or may not have dedicated parking spaces.
  • Location: Common in urban areas, close to amenities.
  • Appeal: Ideal for individuals, couples, or small families, especially in cities.

Bungalow

  • Definition: A single-storey house, typically with a low-pitched roof.
  • Ownership: Usually freehold.
  • Size: Varies, but often smaller than multi-storey homes, typically with 2–3 bedrooms.
  • Floors: Single-story (no stairs).
  • Privacy: High, as there are no shared walls.
  • Parking: Often has a driveway or garage.
  • Location: Common in suburban or rural areas.
  • Appeal: Ideal for people who prefer single-level living (e.g., elderly, those with mobility issues).

Penthouse

  • Definition: A luxury apartment located on the top floor of a building, often with private access.
  • Ownership: Usually freehold or leasehold.
  • Size: Larger than typical apartments, often with expansive living spaces and multiple bedrooms.
  • Floors: Typically single-level, but some have two floors (duplex).
  • Privacy: High, as it's the top floor and often has fewer neighbors.
  • Parking: Often includes private or dedicated parking spaces.
  • Location: Found in high-rise buildings, typically in city centers or upscale urban areas.
  • Appeal: Exclusive, offering panoramic views, privacy, and high-end amenities.
